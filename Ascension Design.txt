ASCENSION

Multiplayer and Singleplayer Building Sandbox

New Earth theme based on scripture

Players are immortal and the world is eternal

Water of life replenishes stamina? (There is no stamina, you can't get tired in the new world - "They will run and not grow weary")

There is no sin, no sickness, no death, no war, no classes, no job requirements, no PVE/PVP, no sorcery, no enchantments, no weapons/armor, no currency

Players gain experience to advance creative ability which includes but is not limited to: Botany/Horticulture/Agriculture
Mining/Metallurgy(Smelting)/Smithing
Lumber/Woodworking/Carpentry
Construction and Clayworks/Stoneworks
Terraforming and Landscaping

Players utilize barter and trade to exchange goods and services

Day/Night counts up based on world birthdate
Seasons count the Moons, Every 7th day is the Sabbath.

Every thing is player made, players can shape and change the world around them.

gathering- every resource can be gathered from the environment
Bushes, Vines and Trees can provide fruits which provide seed
Trees can provide useful materials, wood and sprouts to plant new trees
Rocks, cliffs or caves can provide raw metals, mineral materials or gemstones!
Flowers can be grown or wild flowers searched for objects such as flint, whetstones, pyrite and other useful raw tools.
Wild Grains/Vegetables can provide seed and food ingredients.

farming- resources can be cultivated, grown, harvested 
Gathered fruits, grains or vegetables can provide seeds which are sown to grow more which provide food ingredients or materials
Animals can be tamed and animal husbandry can take place to produce more animals -- these can be used with tools to achieve more productive results (for example, yoking two power animals such as oxen or bulls that can then plow a field with a plowshare)

crafting- resources can be used to create new tools and items
Many tools and objects can be made from resources at hand which provide access to new abilities or improved abilities
Refer to the tech tree for an example layout of what tool provides what ability.
For example, buildables such as a smelter is useful for making ingots, a forge and an anvil are useful for making tools, an oven for baking, etc - tools are required to build
Hammer, Carving Knife, Pickaxe and Shovel are the basic kit.
