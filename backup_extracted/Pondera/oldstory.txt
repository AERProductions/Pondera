"Generations have passed since the Mundus Eversor made its presence known to Modern Civilization.

Those that remained after the destruction had pieced together any resemblance of a life they could, rebuilding into a medieval style era.

Four Principalities, Unknowing of each other, exist in this world to rule their known lands and fight the unknown.

Unfortunately, The Lands have grown tired of Mankind and treat them thusly, driving creatures from the depths and into the world, but Why?
The answer seems to remain within the Crater of the Mundus Eversor, but who dares to traverse such an unholy land?
Only time will provide the answer..."

This is a game of the ages that will be released and steadily maintained.
