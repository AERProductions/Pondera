## How Long Does It Last?

- **7-Day Freeze**: Lasts 7 real-world days (approximately)
- **14-Day Freeze**: Lasts 14 real-world days (approximately)
- **30-Day Freeze**: Lasts 30 real-world days (approximately)

---

## What Happens When Freeze Ends?

1. Your freeze automatically expires
2. Your deed automatically "unfreezes"
3. You have 30 days to pay next maintenance
4. You get 7-day grace period if you can't pay on time
5. Everything back to normal!

## Common Questions

**Q: Will my deed be lost if I freeze it?**  
A: No! Freezing protects your deed. It keeps it safe while you're gone.

**Q: Can members still play while frozen?**  
A: Yes! Your members can continue using the village and harvesting resources.

**Q: What if my break is longer than 30 days?**  
A: Just freeze again before the first freeze ends. You can stack freezes!

**Q: Do I lose resources while frozen?**  
A: No! Resources grow normally. Your village remains fully operational.

**Q: What if I return early?**  
A: Simply unfreeze your deed and you'll have 30 days to pay maintenanceâ€”no penalty.

**Q: What happens if I don't pay after freeze ends?**  
A: You get a 7-day grace period. After that, the deed is lost. But you have 37 days total to prepare!

**Q: Can I freeze multiple times?**  
A: Yes! You can freeze again whenever you need another break.

**Q: Is there a cost to freeze?**  
A: No! Freezing is completely free.
